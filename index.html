Movies TV Shows

You've watched a movie or a TV show and really liked it, only to later find out it was based on a book or a graphical novel. So you find the book, read it, and realize that you like it so much better than the movie. There are so many details that didn't make it into the "moving pictures". O n the other hand, while picturing the scenes, you were seeing the actors, not your vision of characters. And after some time the scenes that are stack in your mind are the visual ones, from the movie, not the ones you envisioned.

So... Before you go and watch someone else's vision, come here and do a search to see if it was based on a "written word". If it was, read the book first and let your imagination build magical worlds that can't compare to any few-hours-long adaptations.
